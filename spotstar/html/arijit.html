ARIJIT SINGH

Arijit Singh is one of the most popular and versatile playback singers in Bollywood, known for his soulful voice and emotive delivery. Rising to fame with the song "Tum Hi Ho" from *Aashiqui 2*, he has since become a dominant figure in Indian music, with a wide repertoire that spans romantic ballads, heart-wrenching melodies, and peppy tracks. His ability to infuse deep emotion into his songs has earned him a massive fan following and numerous awards, making him a staple in modern Indian cinema's music scene.
