ARMAAN MALIK

Armaan Malik is a talented Indian playback singer, songwriter, and actor who has made a significant mark in the Bollywood music industry. Known for his smooth, melodious voice, Armaan has delivered hit songs across various genres, from romantic ballads to upbeat tracks. Starting his career at a young age, he quickly gained recognition with popular songs like "Main Rahoon Ya Na Rahoon" and "Bol Do Na Zara." With his versatility and youthful charm, Armaan has built a strong fanbase and continues to be a prominent voice in contemporary Indian music.
