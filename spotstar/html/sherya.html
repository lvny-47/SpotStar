SHERYA GOSHAL

Shreya Ghoshal is one of India's most celebrated playback singers, known for her versatile and enchanting voice. She made her Bollywood debut with the film *Devdas* and quickly became a sensation with her melodious renditions in various genres, from classical to contemporary music. Shreya's songs, such as "Teri Ore," "Agar Tum Mil Jao," and "Sun Raha Hai," have touched millions of hearts, earning her numerous awards and widespread acclaim. With her impeccable vocal skills and emotional depth, she continues to be a leading figure in the Indian music industry.
